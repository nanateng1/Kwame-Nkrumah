The CPP(Convention People's Party)

Ghana's Govnered First Political Party

Background

CPP(The Convention People's Party) was formed in June 1949 by Kwame Nkrumah . The CPP is socialist political party in Ghana based on the ideologies of the Kwame Nkrumah the first president of Ghana. He was at that time the Secretary General of the United Gold Coast Convention (UGCC).

Ideologies

Kwame Nkrumah founded the CPP on the following ideologies:

  • Nkrumasim
  • Pan-Africanism
  • African Nationlism
  • Left-wing Nationalism
  • Scientific-Socialism
  • Anti-Colonialism
  • Anti-Imperialism

Colors/Motto/Symbol and Structure

The colors of CPP are Red, White and Green.

The motto of the party is FORWARD EVER BACKWARD NEVER.

Its symbol: A red cockerel heralding the dawn.

Party branches were to be established in every town and village, throughout the country. It was to be a mass-based party each branch of which was to be administered by an elected Branch Executive committee. There was to be a National Secretariat under the direct supervision and control of the Central Committee of the party

Importance of CPP in Ghana's History

The Convention's People Party(CPP) may in these times be irrelevant in the sense that currently, there is not a single member of parliament from the CPP. The recent past elections their vote percentage has declined.

Inspite of all these, the CPP's relevance in Ghana's history can not be overlooked. The foundations laid by the founder the first president of the republic till this the day drives the country's economy. Some Ghanaians have even argued that he is the best president Ghana has ever had. A big question is "if the CPP gave Ghana its best president why are the struggling to fetch even 1% vote during elections?"

Some achievments of the CPP which till this day palys a major role in Ghana's Economy

  • Establishment of Atomic Reactor
  • Aluminium Smelter
  • Cocoa Processing Factories
